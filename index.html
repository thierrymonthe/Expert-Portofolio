About me

My strong interest in mathematics and for complex problems naturally led me to the field of data science after a master’s degree in applied mathematics and a master’s degree in Data Science then had the opportunity to perfect my skills and discover numerous branches of the of Data Science field: Data Engineering, Machine Learning, IoT, NLP during multiple projects with clients in various fields (Finance, Banking, Mechanical Engineering, Telecom).

I am the Co-founder of a German Start Up created in 2021 and specialized in development of Data Products, that also works in the Consulting Industry with as well SMEs as global player like DEVK (Insurance), DKB (Bank).

As expressed in our summarized vision From Data to Value, we transform the data potential of our customers into actionable allowing them to optimize their production, their processes and enhance their product portfolio by with new Data Products.

We can therefore support efficiently our clients in their Digital Transformation Process and help them taking the right decision to avoid costly failures.
